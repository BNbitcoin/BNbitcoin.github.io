Privacy policy



The website https://bnbitcoin.org/ (hereinafter, referred to as the "We" or "we") does not collect or share users' personal information.
We do not track and we do not profile users: we avoid to collect personal data because all that it is not collected, it can't also be stolen, leaked or abused.
According the EU regulatory system, which includes the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we consider "personal data" any information about you (user) or your behavior that can be traced back to you as personal data. For this reason, particularly:

-    We don't record your IP address;

-    We don't serve any tracking or identifying cookies;

-    We don't have any analytics implemented;

-    We don't ask users to submit any additional information, including those related to their crypto accounts (it is not possible because we do not have any editable section on the website to enter information)

-    We do not store any data from users, following the principles of privacy by design and privacy by default.

We use external links to websites (social networks, forums, blogs) having their own cookie and privacy policies over which we have no control. Even if we apply a "no-referrer" referrer policy to prevent from leaking referrers and hence from tracking users on the net by linked websites, we are not responsible for any damage caused by these websites.
If you have any questions about our privacy policy please email: admin[at]bnbitcoin.org
