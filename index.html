What is BNbitcoin?

BNbitcoin is the first pure mined PoW token on the Binance Smart Chain network. It is a tokenized version of the original bitcoin project by Satoshi Nakamoto (2009, whitepaper). It derives from the 0xbitcoin project already available on the Ethereum blockchain, but with some features that make BNbitcoin unique and closer to the original bitcoin design.
As bitcoin, there is no airdrop nor ICO: it can only be mined and (soon) traded on the exchanges.

How can I obtain it?

You can obtain BNBTC by mining it.
In the next future it will begin to be available (in terms of liquidity) also for trading on exchanges/swappers.
You can mine it both with CPU or GPU.

Can you provide more details about mining BNbitcoin?

A quick way to start mining is to install Metamask to create an account (exactly like in the case of an Ethereum account), and add the Binance Smart Chain to your networks portfolio, as described here. Now you have to fund your account with BNB on the Smart Chain mainnet: this is needed because of the (low) gas-fees you have to pay when you mint BNBTC tokens after finding a solution and claim the BNBTC reward.
There exist several token mining programs, such as MVis-tokenminer.
In the miner configuration file, you must specify the destination RPC URL (https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org), plus your account address, private key and the BNBTC contract address as target for mining (0xE7Cb24F449973D5B3520E5b93D88B405903c75Fb).
Voilà! You can start mining with CPU or GPU, and after having added BNBTC to your assets on Metamask, you can see (if you win challenges) that your BNBTC amount increases in real time in your wallet.

Why should I mine BNbitcoin instead of other popular mineable tokens on Ethereum?

Because after the DeFi massive widespread on the Ethereum mainnet, gas fees for transactions have become too high. This has a relevant and heavy impact on all the projects, and particularly if considering mined-tokens-based Dapps: claiming rewards has become too expensive.
The alternative is offered by the Smart Chain: with its low gas fees, fastness and smart-contract execution capabilities, it is the most suitable environment to give rebirth to bitcoin through the tokenized minable asset BNBTC.

What is the Binance Smart Chain?

Binance Smart Chain is a Proof-of-Staked-Authority (PoSA) blockchain which enables the creation of smart contracts. Its native currency is BNB, it is EVM-compatible and it creates an ecosystem where validators, token holders, developers, and users all benefit from a rewarding blockchain that offers high performance and ample space for further innovations. It has very low transaction fees and blocks are generated every 3 seconds, making this environment the most suitable for the recent DeFi-revolution applications and for mining and trading BNBTC without worrying too much about the minting/transaction fees.

Can I trade BNbitcoin on the exchanges?

We hope to see as soon as possible trades/swaps for BNBTC, but for sure, at least in the initial phases, the mining activity plays a keyrole to build asset liquidity.

Has the BNbitcoin smart contract been audited?

The original smart contract of 0xbitcoin has been audited and no relevant issues are present. The BNbitcoin contract differs only for some settings (which are restored as in the Satoshi Nakamoto's bitcoin) and for an additional read-only function to make it compatible with BEP-20 standards. For this reason the BNbitcoin contract and code lines can be considered equivalent to 0xbitcoin in terms of safety.
