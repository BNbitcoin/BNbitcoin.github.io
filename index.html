What is BNbitcoin?

BNbitcoin is the first pure-mined Proof-of-Work experimental, BEP20-compliant token on the BNB Chain network. It is inspired by the original Bitcoin project of Satoshi Nakamoto, but programmed to be a mineable token through a smart contract running on the BNB Smart Chain (BSC) network.
It is an open-source, community-driven project with no privileged roles, fairly-launched experiment with no pre-mine, no airdrop nor ICO: it is transparently and solely distributed through Proof-of-Work mining, just like Bitcoin.

Why is BNbitcoin different if compared to other projects?

BNBTC aims to be an innovative experiment, as it represents the first fully BEP-20-compliant token distributed via Proof-of-Work on a Proof-of-Staked-Authority (PoSA) blockchain. It was created in response to the multitude of premined projects that often feature development teams with privileged rolesâ€”either because they initially hold a large portion of the token supply or because they have the ability to manipulate the token distribution rules. With BNBTC, there are no privileged individuals, no official development team, and no roadmap decided by anyone with higher authority. There is no one who can interfere with its neutral, transparent distribution mechanism, which is based solely on the ability to solve a mathematical puzzle. Ultimately, the goal of this experiment is to prove that it is possible to build a truly neutral, community-driven project inspired by Bitcoin's founding valuesâ€”even on EVM networks and regardless of their consensus mechanism.

What is the "BNB Smart Chain"?

The BNB Smart Chain is a Proof-of-Staked-Authority (PoSA) blockchain which enables the creation of smart contracts. Its native currency is BNB, it is EVM-compatible and it creates an ecosystem where validators, developers, and users all benefit from a blockchain that offers high performance and ample space for further innovations. It has very low transaction fees and blocks are generated every few seconds, making this one of the most suitable environments for many Web3 applications.
