Privacy policy


The website https://bnbitcoin.org (hereinafter, referred to as the "We" or "we") does not collect users' personal information.
We do not track and we do not profile users: we avoid to collect personal data because all that it is not collected, it can't also be stolen, leaked or abused.
We consider "personal data" any information about you (user) or your behavior that can be traced back to you as personal data. For this reason, particularly:

- We don't record your IP address;

- We don't serve any tracking or identifying cookies;

- We don't have any analytics implemented;

- We don't ask you to submit any information; 

- We do not store any of your data, following the principles of privacy by design and privacy by default.

This website contains external links and references to other websites (i.e. social networks, forums, blogs, repositories). This privacy policy is applicable only to this website and it can't be used as reference for linked websites, over which we have no control. Even if we apply a strict referrer policy ("no-referrer") to prevent from leaking referrers and hence from tracking users on the net by linked websites, we are not responsible for any damage caused by these websites.
If you have any questions about our privacy policy please email: admin[at]bnbitcoin.org

This privacy policy was amended for the last time on April, 19th, 2021 and may be altered at any time without prior notice.
